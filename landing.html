FAQ

  • Explain this to me in plain English?
    1. We've built a multi-platform file sharing and transfer app that people use and enjoy. It's called StoAmigo. Maybe you've used it!
    2. We're introducing blockchain technology to our app to create a new feature called Paid Link. Essentially, it allows people to sell files with a link - only they don't get money. They get AXEL Tokens instead.
    3. You can exchange AXEL Tokens for local currency, and vice-versa.
    4. We plan to build a searchable marketplace for people who are selling files that anyone can join. A ton of people will sell files on this marketplace, giving you a lot of stuff to buy with your tokens.
  • Why use tokens instead of cash?

    The AXEL Token is an encrypted, completely secure method of transaction built off of our blockchain technology. You'll be able to buy tokens with your local currency. Because it's built with blockchain, it allows several incredible advantages over cash. Instant transactions, built-in IP protection, total privacy – and quite a few other things.

  • Why use blockchain?

    Blockchain allows for built-in encryption and a level of decentralization that is absolutely critical to the peer-to-peer system we're trying to build. Blockchain is the natural next step in our mission of giving people back control of their stuff.

  • Why are you so confident you'll get tons of sellers?

    Because StoAmigo will be the best way of selling out there. It's incredibly difficult and expensive to sell files today. There are usually startup costs involved for setting up a storefront, as well as complicated integrations and coding. Not to mention the percentages third parties take for hosting the files and helping the audience find you. With StoAmigo, you don't even need a website. You can start selling in minutes, with no real instructions necessary, without spending anything.

  • Where can I buy these tokens?

    You'll get some tokens just for being an active StoAmigo user. There will be a way to buy AXEL Tokens with your local currency - but it's not here yet. Sign up to the mailing list and we'll let you know when it's here!

  • When is all this happening?

    We're aiming for the second half of 2018 for the StoAmigo Marketplace... but we have some really cool developments before that. If all goes well, you should be able to use your AXEL Tokens in the next few months.

  • What are these tokens worth?

    Right now, they're worth what people think they're worth. The value of each token will depend, in the long term, on how many people are buying and selling things on the StoAmigo Marketplace. The more people use them, the more in-demand they will be.
